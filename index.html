About Us

Open since 1995, Silver Lake Yoga is the oldest Yoga studio in Silverlake and among the oldest studios in Los Angeles. Our goal is to provide excellent yoga instruction in a relaxed, friendly environment. Call it Yoga without the attitude. Whether you’re coming to class to stay in shape, relieve stress, address chronic aches and pains, or to connect to a deeper part of yourself, everyone is welcome at Silverlake Yoga!

We offer over sixty classes a week. These include classes for both beginners and advanced students, Flow classes, Prenatal classes, Mommy & Me classes, Kids’ Yoga, Pilates mat classes, and Tai Chi. Class size is generally 10 to 12 students. A few of our more popular evening and weekend classes can attract up to 25. No preregistration is required. Just show up a few minutes ahead of time for class and wear comfortable clothes that you can move in. We will provide yoga mats.
